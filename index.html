Section-by-section

Detailed resume feedback

Every section of your resume gets a margin note — what's working, and what to fix next.

Contact Information

Good

Email and phone number are clearly placed at the top.

Fix

Add a LinkedIn and GitHub/portfolio link — recruiters check these first.

Technical Skills

Good

Skills are grouped by category (languages, frameworks, tools).

Fix

List skills in order of relevance to the target role, not alphabetically.

Projects

Good

Each project lists the tech stack used.

Fix

Add measurable outcomes — "reduced load time by 40%" beats "optimized performance".

Education

Good

Degree, college, and graduation year are all present and correctly ordered.

Fix

Add CGPA only if it's above your institute's typical placement cutoff.

Achievements

Good

Hackathon and competitive programming ranks are included.

Fix

Mention scale — "Top 5 of 400 teams" reads stronger than "Won hackathon".

Certifications

Good

Certifications are relevant to the applied role.

Fix

Drop outdated or unrelated certificates to keep the section tight.

Resume Formatting

Good

Single-column layout — safe for ATS parsers.

Fix

Resume runs to 1.5 pages — trim to a clean single page for entry-level roles.

Before you ask

Frequently asked questions

What exactly is an ATS score?

An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) score estimates how well software used by recruiters can read and rank your resume. A higher score means fewer parsing errors and better keyword alignment with the job description.

Which file formats are supported?

Currently PDF, DOC, and DOCX, capped at 5MB per upload. Image-based or scanned PDFs may not parse correctly since most ATS systems can't read them either.

How does the AI generate suggestions?

The analysis pipeline parses your resume into sections, compares them against patterns common in shortlisted resumes for your target role, and highlights gaps in keywords, formatting, and measurable impact.

Is my resume data kept private?

This build is a front-end preview with no backend yet, so nothing is uploaded or stored anywhere. Once the backend ships, uploaded resumes will be processed for scoring only and not shared with third parties.

How accurate is the job matching?

Match percentage is based on overlap between your listed skills and a role's required skills. It's a strong first filter, but always read the full job description before applying or skipping a role.

Can I re-analyze after editing my resume?

Yes — once the backend is live, you'll be able to re-upload an updated resume as many times as you want to track your score improvement over time.
